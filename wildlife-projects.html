Explore Uganda's Wildlife

Mammals

From lions to chimpanzees, Uganda is home to a diverse range of mammal species found in its national parks and reserves.

Birds

With over 1,000 bird species, Uganda is a haven for birdwatchers and a sanctuary for rare and endangered birds.

Reptiles

Uganda's wetlands and forests are home to unique reptiles, including the Nile crocodile and various snake species.

Conservation Projects

Our projects include anti-poaching patrols, habitat restoration, and community engagement to protect wildlife and promote sustainability.
