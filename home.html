About UWCS

The Uganda Wildlife Conservation Society (UWCS) is committed to protecting the rich biodiversity of Uganda. From lush savannahs to thriving wetlands, our work ensures that endangered species like mountain gorillas, African elephants, and rare bird species are safeguarded for future generations.

We achieve this through innovative conservation projects, collaboration with communities, and education programs that inspire action worldwide.
